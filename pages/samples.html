Tiyulim and Special Programs

Tiyulim

In addition to delving into Hashem’s Torah, Ma’ayanot is serious about our students’ connection with Hashem’s Land. Our students go on approximately 25 tiyulim (trips) per year, exploring many of the most holy, historical, and fun places across Eretz Yisrael. These tiyulim help foster a deep and lasting familiarity and love of the Land that our students take with them for the rest of their lives.
