About Mendy Wax

One of my greatest joys in life is music. I play the piano, keyboard, and acoustic guitar. I've been providing music for people's simchas as a One Man Band and Singer for more then 17 years.

I play at weddings, bar mitzvahs, and all other simchas. I am well known for making people's simchas lively, while playing at a comfortable decible. My motto is "Your guests will go home singing and dancing, but not hard of hearing!" Another unique feature I offer is a kumzits at your simcha, which can also be booked as a stand alone option.

I have given music lessons to hundreds of children. I have an original teaching method that I have developed which combines learning to play from sheet music and by ear. This enables my students to continue to develop their musical skills after finishing their lessons. I have a small home studio where I have recorded various small productions from demo CDs to camp recordings. One boy recorded some of his original compositions and gave out the CDs at his Bar Mitzvah.

I produced my album, Hodu L'Hashem, several years ago. It is now available for free download on the Mendy's Album page. The songs are all my own compositions. Yisroel Lamm did the arrangements and they came out top notch! I had a lot of fun making the album and I hope you enjoy it, as well.

I currently live in Ramat Beit Shemesh, Israel, with my wife and four children.
