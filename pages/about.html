About Ma'ayanot

Our Mission

Learn More

Rigorous Torah Learning

At Ma’ayanot, you will encounter high-level, textual Torah learning in a vibrant beit midrash and dynamic, rigorous classes. You will be challenged by your teachers and encouraged by our warm, approachable staff.

Open and Supportive Atmosphere

At Ma’ayanot, you will be immersed in an atmosphere that encourages serious growth in Torah, middot, and yir’at shamayim, along with questioning and discussion. Our warm, approachable staff is open to questions and discussion about the material you are learning or any issue that is important to you.

Multidimensional Learning

At Ma’ayanot we seek to educate each student as a “whole person,” who has a variety of interests and seeks to find new and meaningful applications of Torah learning and Torah living. Explore and grow through Torah with your mind, body, and soul as you encounter the intersection of Torah and art, psychology, creative writing, leadership skills, Israel advocacy, and life-skills.
